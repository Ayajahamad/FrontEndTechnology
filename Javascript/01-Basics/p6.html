OPERATORS

Jvascript operators are symbols that are used to perform operations on operand.

        1. Arithmetic Operations => Addition +, Subtraction -, Multiplication *, Division /, Modulus %, Increment ++, Decrement --

        2. Comparison (Relation) Operators => Is equal to ==, Identical (equal and of same data type) ===, Not equal to !=. Not Identical !==, Greater than >,Greater equal to >=, Less than <, Less than or equal to <=

        3. Bitwise Operators => Bitwise AND &, Bitwise OR |, Bitwise XOR ^, Bitwise NOT ~, Bitwise Left Shift <<, Bitwise Right Shift >>, Bitwise Right shift with Zero >>>

        4. Logical Operators => Logical AND &&, Logical OR ||, Logical Not ! 

        5. Assignment Operators => Assign =, Add and Assign +=, Substract and assign -=, Multiply and assign *=, Divide and assign /=, Modulus and assign %=

        6. Special or Ternary Operators => condition ? "true":"false";
